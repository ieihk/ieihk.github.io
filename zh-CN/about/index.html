关于

任务

Our mission is to connect entrepreneurs with people who can improve their businesses.

愿景

Our vision is to build a network of entrepreneurs who help each other become successful.

历史

Founded in 2011 by Professor Danny Chau, the International Entrepreneurs Institute serves the purpose of bringing business people together to help each other become successful. We believe that by helping each other and synergising our skills and talents, we can all succeed together.

We have a growing network of 50 entrepreneurs so we will be able to get you in touch with whatever you need. If you need a video done or want some advice on a particular area of business, we can put you in touch with the right people.
